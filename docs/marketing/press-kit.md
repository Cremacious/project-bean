```
Bedtime Quests is an interactive bedtime story app for young children, read aloud by a parent. It turns story time into a calm ritual you build together, one goodnight at a time.

You read the scene aloud, your little one chooses what happens next, and their own first name is woven right into the story, so they become the hero of every tale. Every choice leads somewhere new, so each quest can be read again and again, with a different happy ending to discover each time.

The app is made to fit how your family reads, with four text sizes, a choice of reading fonts including a dyslexia friendly option, and both a read to me mode and an I can read mode. Every screen uses warm, high contrast colors so words stay easy to see.

Bedtime Quests is built to be safe for young children. It personalizes stories using a first name only, with no chat, no third party ads, and no third party tracking. The library grows with new quests every month.

Choose your own goodnight.
```

## Key features and benefits

- **Your child stars by name.** Their first name is woven into every story, so they are the hero.
- **They choose the path.** Every choice leads somewhere new, so each quest is replayable with a different happy ending.
- **Read aloud together.** Short, cozy scenes made to be shared at bedtime, with a read to me mode and an I can read mode for new readers.
- **Made to read your way.** Four text sizes and a choice of reading fonts, including a clear high legibility option and a dyslexia friendly OpenDyslexic option.
- **Safe and COPPA friendly.** First name only. No chat, no third party ads, and no third party tracking of your child.
- **Calm by design.** Warm, high contrast art and gentle, happy endings made to help little ones wind down.
- **Always growing.** New quests added every month.

## How to refer to us

- **Spelling and capitalization:** it is **Bedtime Quests** (two words, both capitalized).
  Not "BedTime Quests", "Bedtimequests", or "Bedtime Quest". The full form for headlines is
  **Bedtime Quests: Interactive Bedtime Stories**.
- **Slogan:** Choose your own goodnight.
- **One-line description:** "an interactive bedtime story app where your child chooses the path and stars by name."
- **Logo do:** use the supplied files as is; keep clear space around the mark; place the mark
  on the navy `#16283A` night sky or a light background with enough contrast.
- **Logo don't:** do not recolor, rotate, stretch, add effects to, or redraw the mark; do not
  crop the paper boat out; do not place it on a low contrast or busy background.
- **Voice:** warm, calm, and parent facing. **No dashes** in any copy about us (write "Ages 2
  to 4", "read aloud", "COPPA friendly"). Keep claims honest: it is an interactive story app,
  not a game or a phonics program.
