## 1. Who we are

Bedtime Quests is an interactive bedtime story app for young children, read
aloud by a parent or guardian. It is operated by [COMPANY NAME] ("we", "us",
"our"). You can reach us about privacy at **[SUPPORT EMAIL]** or by mail at
[COMPANY ADDRESS].

This policy explains what information we collect, why, who we share it with, and
the choices you have. Because our app is meant for young children, we designed
it to collect as little about a child as possible.

## 2. A note for parents (this is a child directed app)

Bedtime Quests is directed to children under 13, so we follow the U.S.
Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). The important points:

- The **parent or guardian** creates and controls the account. Children do not
  create accounts and do not enter their own information.
- About a child, we store **only a first name** (so the story can greet them),
  plus reading preferences and which story endings they have found.
- We do **not** knowingly collect a child's last name, birthdate, age, photos,
  voice, precise location, email, phone number, or any other contact details.
- Parents can review and delete their child's information at any time (see
  section 8).

## 3. Information we collect

### 3a. Information about your child

- **First name.** You, the parent, enter your child's first name so the story
  can use it. We store the first name only.
- **Reading preferences.** How the story is shown: read to me or can read, font,
  and text size.
- **Story progress.** Which story endings your child has reached, so we can show
  what they have discovered.

We do not ask your child to type anything about themselves, and we do not
collect a child's last name, age, birthdate, photo, voice, location, or contact
information.

### 3b. Information about you, the parent

- **Account details.** Your name and email address.
- **Sign in credentials.** If you use email and password, we store a secure hash
  of your password, never the password itself. If you use Google or Apple sign
  in, we store the login token that provider gives us, and possibly a profile
  image if the provider returns one.
- **Session and security data.** When you are signed in we record basic session
  information such as your IP address and browser type to keep you logged in and
  to protect the account from abuse.

### 3c. Purchases

Subscriptions are planned through the app stores (Apple and Google) using a
subscription tool called RevenueCat. When you subscribe, the purchase happens
through your app store account. **We never see or store your card number.** We
receive a confirmation that a subscription is active, tied to your adult
account, not to your child.

### 3d. Advertising and analytics identifiers

If we show ads or measure app usage, the ad and analytics providers may create
technical identifiers. We require these to be configured in a **child directed,
limited** mode: **contextual ads only, no behavioral or interest based
advertising, and no ad personalization.** See sections 5 and 6.

## 4. How we use information

- To personalize the story with your child's first name.
- To remember reading preferences and story progress.
- To create and secure your account and keep you signed in.
- To send you account email such as password resets.
- To provide and manage subscriptions.
- To measure and improve the app in a privacy protective way.
- To show **contextual** (non behavioral) ads, if ads are enabled.
- To keep the service safe and prevent abuse.

We do **not** use your child's information to build advertising profiles, and we
do not sell personal information.

## 5. Advertising

If Bedtime Quests shows ads, the ads are **contextual only**. That means they are
based on the content of the app, not on tracking your child or building a
profile of them. We do not permit behavioral or interest based advertising to
children. The ad provider [AD NETWORK NAME] is configured for child directed
treatment, which turns off personalized ads. We do not share your child's first
name or profile with the ad provider.

## 6. Analytics

If we use analytics ([ANALYTICS PROVIDER]) to understand how the app is used, we
configure it for a child directed audience: advertising features and ad
personalization are turned off, and we do not use it to build cross service
profiles of your child. We aim to keep analytics aggregate and non identifying,
and we do not send your child's first name to the analytics provider.

## 7. Who we share information with

We do not sell personal information. We share information only with service
providers that help us run the app, and only for that purpose:

- **[COMPANY NAME] infrastructure providers**: our database host (Neon) stores
  the data; our email provider (Resend) delivers account email such as password
  resets; our web host serves the app.
- **RevenueCat**: to manage subscriptions, using an identifier tied to your
  adult account, never your child's name.
- **[AD NETWORK NAME]**: only if ads are enabled, in contextual, child directed
  mode.
- **[ANALYTICS PROVIDER]**: only if analytics is enabled, in a limited, child
  directed configuration.
- **Legal reasons**: if required by law, or to protect the safety and rights of
  users and the public.

## 8. Parental rights and choices

As a parent or guardian, you can:

- **Review** the information we hold about your child. Contact
  **[SUPPORT EMAIL]** and we will help.
- **Delete** your child's information. You can remove a child profile in the app,
  or delete your whole account, which erases your child's first name and story
  progress along with the account. You can also email **[SUPPORT EMAIL]** to
  request deletion.
- **Withdraw consent.** You can stop our further collection of your child's
  information at any time. If you do, we will stop collecting and delete the
  child's information on request. Note that withdrawing consent may mean parts of
  the app no longer work.
- **Update** your account details from the app settings.

Some of these actions are protected by a parental check to confirm an adult is
present.

## 9. Data retention

We keep information only as long as we need it to run the app for your family.
When you delete a child profile or your account, we delete the associated child
first name and story progress. We remove data from active systems promptly and
from backups within a reasonable period. We may keep limited records where the
law requires it.

## 10. Security

We protect information with reasonable safeguards: passwords are stored only as
secure hashes, connections use encryption in transit, sign in endpoints are rate
limited against abuse, and access to production data is restricted. No system is
perfectly secure, but we work to protect your family's information.

## 11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app changes. When we make a material change, we
will update the effective date above and, where appropriate, notify parents. If
a change expands how we use a child's information, we will obtain parental
consent as required.
